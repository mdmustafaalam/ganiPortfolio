🌏 3D Modeling & Extraction Services

I specialize in creating high-quality 3D models for the Blockbuster mod and Skin & Bones models with realistic animations for Minecraft. My expertise extends to extracting 3D models from games like Roblox, Garry’s Mod, GTA 5 and others, ensuring you get the assets you need.

🛠️ Services Offered:

  • Custom 3D Models for Blockbuster Mod – Fully rigged characters with moving arms and legs.
  • Skin & Bones Models – Realistic animations for characters, optimized for the SNB mod.
  • Game Asset Extraction – Need a model from a game? I can find and extract assets from Roblox, Garry’s Mod, and more.
